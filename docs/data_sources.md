# Data Sources

Suggested public sources for future calibration:

- Epoch AI: AI supercomputer and GPU cluster data.
- International Energy Agency: Energy and AI, data-centre electricity consumption, and energy-system constraints.
- European Commission: AI Continent Action Plan and AI Factories.
- European Commission: energy performance of data centres and Energy Efficiency Directive reporting.
- ENTSO-E Transparency Platform: load, generation, transmission, and market data.
- Eurostat: non-household electricity prices.
- Open Power System Data: cleaned European power-system time series.
- ENTSO-E TYNDP: grid and power-system scenarios.
- Carnegie Endowment and other public analysis on data-centre economics and time-to-power.
